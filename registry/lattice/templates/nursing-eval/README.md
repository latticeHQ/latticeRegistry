# Nursing Clinical Competency Feedback Template

Non-provisioned evaluation template for assessing nursing clinical competency from session transcripts.

## Standards

- **AACN Essentials** — American Association of Colleges of Nursing core competencies
- **QSEN** — Quality and Safety Education for Nurses competencies
- **Benner's Novice to Expert** — Skill acquisition model for performance classification
- **SBAR** — Situation-Background-Assessment-Recommendation communication framework
- **Tanner's Clinical Judgment Model** — Noticing, Interpreting, Responding, Reflecting

## Analysis Passes

| Pass | Weight | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| Patient Assessment | 25% | Systematic assessment, vital signs, pain scales, NANDA-I nursing diagnosis |
| Clinical Judgment | 25% | Tanner's model, priority setting, deterioration recognition, delegation |
| Safety & Quality | 20% | Medication 5 Rights, fall prevention, infection control, SBAR handoffs |
| Communication | 15% | Therapeutic communication, patient education, cultural sensitivity |
| Professionalism | 10% | ANA Code of Ethics, scope of practice, documentation, EBP |
| Synthesis | 5% | Overall score, Benner level classification, clinical learning plan |

## Score Thresholds (Benner's Model)

| Score | Level |
|-------|-------|
| 9.0+  | Expert |
| 7.5+  | Proficient |
| 6.0+  | Competent |
| 4.0+  | Advanced Beginner |
| 0-4.0 | Novice |
